Travel Agent’s Module

URL:

Purpose:

Purpose of this module is to allow travel agents to –

1)      View RFQs for air tickets, as requested by Company

2)      Submit quotes to Company , for options available that conform to the RFQ

3)      Once purchase is approved, upload the e-ticket

4)      Update status if a ticket has been used or the PAX has travelled or not

5)      Upload invoices.

6)      View List of Refunds applied/closed for tickets purchased by Company

Usage:

New Requests:

a)      Any time a new request for quote is submitted by Company, the travel Agent (TA) will receive an email indicating this request.

b)      You can then logon to the Jibe module (url given above) using the userID/password given in the same email.

c)       You will be greeted with the below screen, with option QUOTE PENDING selected. This will show all the RFQs that have been sent by SMS and you have not submitted the quotes to SMS.

 

Submit Quotes:

d)      For the relevant RFQ row, click on the ‘Airplane’ icon under the column ACTIONS (extreme-right) of each row.

e)      If you are prompted to allow/block POP-UP, then select ALWAYS ALLOW for Company.

 

f)       Clicking on the airplane icon, will open the Quote-Submission window as shown below.

 

g)      This screen will display:

·         names/details of the PAX that will be travelling,

·         Route, travel date/time, Economy/Business class

·         Name of the Company PIC

·         Any remarks pout by SMS personnel

·         Your “Operating Currency” as registered with SMS.

 

h)      Click on the ADD NEW QUOTATION button

i)        Another prompt will display, asking you to put the following details of your proposed offer/flight:

·         FLIGHT NUMBER

·         FROM airport

·         TO airport

·         Departure/Arrival Date/time

·         Locator, Class and Status.

·         Remarks, if any.

j)        Click on ADD FLIGHT button

k)      Update the other details of this option – GDS LOCATOR, DEADLINE (date/time), FARE, TAX

l)        You can paste a typical AMADEUS PNR TEXT in the given space as well.

 

 

 

m)  For an option which has a stop-over (more than one flight), you can add another flight in this window.

 

n)      Once it is done, click on button SAVE AND CLOSE.

 

o)      The main screen will now look like below:

 

p)      You can edit the quote, by using the button EDIT for an option.

q)      For another Airline/option, click on the button ADD NEW QUOTATION and continue as per above.

r)       Once you are ready to send your quote to SMS, press the button SEND QUOTE TO Company for one/more options in the above screen.

 

 

 

Approval / Declined / Uploading e-Ticket:

 

Once a purchase of ticket is approved by Company, you will be notified by an email indicating same.

Subsequently, you can upload relevant e-ticket for an approved purchase, by using the E-Ticket button under column ACTIONS, on extreme right of the purchase.

The below window will open.

 

 

 

Update the ticket number, then click on BROWSE button to point to the eTicket File on your computer, and press the UPDATE button. Similarly upload relevant eTickets for all the personnel.

 

Once the eTickets are uploaded, this record will shift to section TICKET ISSUED.

 

For an option declined by Company, it will automatically be shifted to category REQUESTS DECLINED.
